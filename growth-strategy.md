# Growth Strategy — Nemesis Craft Market

## Product Overview

Nemesis Craft Market is a handmade marketplace platform focused on creator activation. The product helps handmade creators such as crochet artists, jewelry makers, nail artists, and accessory sellers publish products and manage listings in a simple mobile-first marketplace.

The primary Sprint 1 goal is increasing creator listings.

Primary activation event:

`listing_completed`

A creator is considered activated when they successfully publish their first handmade product listing.

---

# Activation Metric

A creator is activated when they successfully publish one handmade product listing within 10 minutes of opening the app for the first time.

Activation threshold:
- 1 completed listing

Time window:
- within 10 minutes of first app open

Why this matters:

The core value of Nemesis Craft Market only exists when creators successfully upload products. A marketplace without creator listings has no value for buyers. The listing creation flow is therefore the most important activation behavior in Sprint 1.

The activation event fired is:

`listing_completed`

---

# Acquisition Channels

## Channel 1 — Instagram Handmade Creator Content

Type:
Organic

Why this channel fits:

Most beginner handmade sellers already promote their products through Instagram pages, reels, and stories. Handmade creators frequently discover selling platforms through aesthetic creator content and reposts from other small businesses.

This channel fits the product because:
- the audience already exists on Instagram
- handmade content performs visually well
- creators share products naturally
- product images work well for short-form content

Sprint 2 approach:
- post handmade creator showcase content
- publish product creation reels
- repost creator listings
- use creator-focused hashtags

Priority:
Highest priority because it directly targets the exact audience already selling handmade products online.

---

## Channel 2 — TikTok Craft Content

Type:
Organic / Viral

Why this channel fits:

Craft-related TikTok videos regularly receive strong engagement. Small creators often search TikTok for packaging ideas, crochet inspiration, jewelry trends, and selling advice.

Nemesis can attract creators by showing:
- listing creation process
- product showcases
- behind-the-scenes creator content
- small business packaging videos

Why lower priority than Instagram:

Instagram currently aligns better with portfolio-style handmade selling. TikTok is more discovery-focused and less stable for long-term audience retention.

Priority:
Second priority because it can generate reach quickly but conversion consistency is less predictable.

---

## Channel 3 — University Creator Communities

Type:
Organic

Why this channel fits:

Many students already create handmade products for extra income. University Discord servers, Telegram groups, and Instagram group chats allow direct access to beginner creators with low acquisition cost.

This channel works well because:
- creators trust peer recommendations
- student creators need beginner-friendly selling tools
- the platform is simple enough for first-time sellers

Sprint 2 approach:
- share creator success examples
- promote marketplace launch inside student communities
- recruit first creators manually

Priority:
Third priority because audience size is smaller but highly targeted.

---

# Why Other Channels Are Lower Priority

## Paid Ads

Paid acquisition is lower priority because:
- the MVP is still early-stage
- activation flow is still being validated
- CAC would likely be too high for Sprint 1

The team prefers validating organic creator behavior before spending on ads.

---

## Sales Outreach

Direct sales outreach is unnecessary during Sprint 1 because the platform targets individual creators rather than businesses requiring enterprise onboarding.

---

# North Star Metric

North Star Metric:

Number of completed creator listings per week.

Why this metric matters:

Every successful creator listing increases:
- marketplace inventory
- buyer variety
- marketplace trust
- creator adoption

This metric directly reflects whether the marketplace is becoming more useful.
